Threat Hunter Challenge

The VMware Threat Hunting Challenge (THC) is an interactive Capture-the-Flag (CTF) style game that gives participants exposure to threat hunting exercises using the VMware security solutions such as Carbon Black, NSX, etc. During the event, participants will work against the clock to answer questions, score points and discover who among them is the best threat hunter or team of threat hunters.
